## How to Install

1. Download the latest version of HazeOver for macOS.  
2. Open the DMG installer and move HazeOver.app to the Applications folder.  
3. Launch the app and allow it through macOS Gatekeeper if required.

## Overview

<a href="#">HazeOver for macOS</a> is designed to help you focus on the task at hand. It dims background windows to reduce distractions and keeps your attention on the window that matters. This is especially useful when working on important tasks, where interruptions can severely impact productivity. By automatically blurring everything except your current window, HazeOver helps you create a clean workspace and focus on the job that needs your attention.

It works by applying a soft dim effect to all non-active windows, making the active window pop out. You can customize the level of dimming and even set HazeOver to automatically start at login. The app integrates seamlessly with macOS, working in the background without requiring much attention from the user. With a simple interface and customizable settings, it's perfect for anyone who needs to maintain a clear mental state while working on their Mac.

Whether you're writing, coding, or designing, HazeOver's focus-enhancing capabilities can help you eliminate distractions and maximize your productivity. It’s a simple yet powerful tool that’s easy to set up and use, making it ideal for anyone who needs to stay in the zone.
